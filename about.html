All about NailsByMbalz

NailsByMbalz is proudly based in Vosloorus, offering high-quality nail care services with a personal touch.

With a passion for beauty and creativity, the nail tech behind NailsByMbalz brings a fresh perspective to every set.

The business was founded with the goal of empowering clients through self-expression and confidence.

Certified and trained, the nail technician ensures hygiene, professionalism, and the latest in nail trends.

Every appointment is an opportunity to create something beautiful, detailed, and uniquely tailored to each client.
